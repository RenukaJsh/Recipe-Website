GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID
  • Flour
  • Sooji/Rava
  • Baking Powder
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup. Add cardamom
  2. Add the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough.
  3. Gently roll into round Gulab Jamuns. Fry
  4. Add the fried Gulab Jamuns to the prepared sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm.
